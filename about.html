Kelsey Olivia is a mixed media artist currently residing in Brooklyn, NY. Moving around a lot from a young age, Kelsey became accustomed to change early on, realizing that in life it is the only constant. Her work is inspired by this ongoing theme. Many of her paintings are based off of lost and found photographs, depicting a memory from a time or place. They are but a thing of the past; the only way to hold onto these memories is to accept these changes and faded memories and turn them into something new.
